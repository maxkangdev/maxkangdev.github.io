#### Why now?🤔 Wouldn't AI make writing obsolete? 🤖

Well, I thought so too... but this essay [writes and write-nots](https://paulgraham.com/writes.html) changed my mind.  
>The author predicts that, with AI, few people will know how to write well in the future. Writing requires clear thinking, but many will rely on AI instead. This could divide society into those who write—and think critically—and those who don’t. Only those who choose to write will maintain deep thinking skills. — Summarized by AI  

After reading the essay, I knew for sure that I would end up as a **write-not**.  
I am already outsourcing all the email drafts to LLMs. Also, look.... I was even too lazy to write the summary of essay myself.

So I will write... at least try as long as possible this time.
