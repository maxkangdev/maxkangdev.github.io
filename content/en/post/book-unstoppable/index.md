## Thoughts and Impressions

Frankly, I don't remember much of the content... (I didn't pay too much attention while reading) 😓 But, like most self-help books these days, the key messages seemed to be similar:

- Keep learning.
- Stop making excuses.
- Step out of your comfort zone.
- Don’t settle for the status quo.
- Live a healthy and balanced life.
- Treat the people around you well.

plus sleep well, exercise, meditate, read books, eat healthily, and reduce screen time... etc  you know... 

In the end, the value of a self-help book depends on how effectively it conveys these messages and how well it resonates with the readers.   
From that perspective, this book wasn’t bad.


## Recommended Readers

If you’re feeling a bit demotivated in life, I think this book is worth reading at least once.

However, I recommend reading it in one go and immersing yourself.  
If you read it too sporadically, the motivation might fade away before it fully sinks in, resetting your progress. (Like me... 🤣)


## Not Recommended for

If you already know these messages to some extent and are actively working to live by them, reading a self-help book might feel like a waste of time.

In that case, I’d suggest reading a book on economics or something related to your profession instead—it might be more beneficial.

(As someone who believes they’re already trying to live this way,) I plan to take a break from self-help books after this one.
